About Me

Hello, I'm Nazif Keyan, a passionate and driven Web Developer and UI/UX Designer from Bangladesh. At just 15 years old, I’ve already begun crafting seamless, visually captivating websites and interactive applications. I specialize in combining elegant design with functional code, ensuring every user interaction is smooth and intuitive. I am constantly learning and experimenting with cutting-edge technologies, especially in AI development. From building local AI chatbots with powerful models to creating immersive 3D games, my curiosity drives me to explore new fields and push the boundaries of what’s possible. Whether it's a portfolio website, a weather app, or an advanced AI system, I believe in delivering experiences that are not only visually appealing but also technically flawless. In my free time, you’ll likely find me playing video games, diving into programming tutorials, or exploring new AI models. Let's build something extraordinary together!
